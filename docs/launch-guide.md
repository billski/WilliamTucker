### 3. The "First Client" Strategy

**Step 1: Free Assessments (2-3)**

> "I'm launching my consultancy and offering free AI assessments to my first 3 clients."

Be upfront. People respect honesty.

**Step 2: Testimonials**

- Real quotes for your website
- Case studies with permission
- Portfolio proof

**Step 3: Paid Clients**

- Referrals from free assessment clients
- Repeat work from satisfied clients
- Credibility from published case studies

**Your first client will almost certainly come from someone you already know.** Tell everyone in your network what you're doing -- friends, former colleagues, family friends who run businesses. The most common path:

> Someone you know says "oh, my friend's company was just talking about AI..."

## Things You're Not Thinking About

### Legal & Business Setup

| Priority | Task | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Register sole proprietorship | ~$40 | BC Registries online |
| 2 | Business bank account | Free | Keep personal/biz separate from day one |
| 3 | Consulting contract template | $0-300 | Template online or pay a lawyer |
| 4 | Professional liability insurance | $500-1K/yr | Some clients will require it |
| 5 | Professional email | Included | william@williamtucker.ca via Dreamhost |

### Employment Check

- **Review your employment contract** for non-compete or moonlighting clauses
- Most won't block consulting in a different sector, but know what you signed
- **Never** use your employer's name, clients, or systems in marketing without permission

### Pricing

| Service | Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Discovery call | Free (30 min) | Top of funnel |
| AI Assessment | $5K - $10K | 2-3 week engagement |
| Implementation / PoC | $15K - $40K | 4-8 weeks, project-based |
| Fractional AI Advisor | $5K - $10K/mo | Ongoing retainer |
| Workshop / Training | $3K - $5K/day | Half-day or full-day |

**Rule:** Practice saying your rate out loud until it feels normal. Your first instinct will be to charge too little. Don't.
