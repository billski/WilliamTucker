How Each Billing Model Creates Invoices

Fixed Price
Milestone-based or on completion. You create a single line item for the agreed amount (e.g., "AI Assessment — $7,500"). Can split into multiple invoices for large projects (50% upfront, 50% on delivery).
Monthly Retainer
Recurring monthly invoice. Auto-generate each month with the retainer amount as a single line item. One click to create, review, and send. Time entries tracked separately for your records.
Day Rate
Invoice pulls billable time entries for the period. Line items show each day worked with description. Total = days × daily rate. You review and adjust before sending.

Technical Implementation:
PDF Generation: Server-side using React-PDF or html-to-pdf. Generated on demand, stored in Supabase Storage.
Email: Resend (or similar) for transactional email — invoice sent notification with PDF attachment + portal link.
Stripe Integration: Stripe Checkout session created per invoice. Webhook confirms payment → auto-updates invoice status. No need for subscriptions — each invoice is a one-time payment link.
GST/HST: Tax rate configurable in Settings (default 0%). When you register, flip it to 5% (GST) or your applicable rate. Applied to all new invoices. Your GST# appears on invoice when set.
Overdue Detection: Cron job (Vercel cron or Supabase edge function) checks daily. Invoices past due_date with status "sent" → marked "overdue". Optional email reminder to client.
