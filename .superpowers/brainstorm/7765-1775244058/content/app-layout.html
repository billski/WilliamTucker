All Pages

Every page in the admin app

Dashboard
Revenue stats, pipeline value, hours/utilization, upcoming follow-ups, recent invoices, overdue alerts
Contacts
List/filter all contacts by status. Click into full profile: details, activity timeline, linked proposals/projects/invoices
Contact Detail
Full client profile — identity, business info, tags, activity log, linked proposals, projects, invoices, documents. Add notes, log interactions.
Proposals
List all proposals by status. Create new from template (per service type). Send, track acceptance, convert to project.
Projects
Active engagements with budget tracking, deliverables checklist, hours logged vs budget, billing model display.
Time Tracking
Weekly timesheet view. Log hours by project, mark billable/non-billable. Timer option for real-time tracking.
Invoices
Create from project/time entries, apply GST/HST, generate PDF, email to client. Track payment status.
Expenses
Log expenses by category, upload receipts, link to projects. Filter by date/category for tax time.
Documents
Central file storage. Upload contracts, deliverables, receipts. Toggle client visibility for portal.
Settings
Business info (name, address, GST# when ready), invoice defaults (payment terms, numbering), tax rates, email templates.
